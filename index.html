FAQ

The questions every agent asks first.

Day 0 you record. Day 2 you see your avatar. Day 3 your first video publishes. If you want to review scripts before publishing (recommended for the first 2 weeks), add about 15 minutes per week. After that, zero.

Three layers. The prompt itself is guardrailed against Fair Housing-violating language at generation, so protected-class terms literally can't come out. A real person on our QC team reviews every single video before it publishes. And for your first two weeks, you personally approve every script. Turn that off when you're comfortable. Turn it back on anytime.

Your voice clone is built from 20+ minutes of your actual speech, and the script generator writes in your syntax — not generic real-estate copy. Every video passes through a human QC reviewer before publishing. If it sounds like a robot, it doesn't ship. You also keep script veto during your first two weeks — flip the kill switch back on anytime.

Most CA brokerages are fine with AI-assisted content as long as a human is in the loop and the post carries AI disclosure — both of which we do by default on every platform. If your broker has a specific content policy, tell us during onboarding and we'll either adjust disclosure language or route every video to your broker for pre-publish approval. We build around your brokerage's compliance framework, not around ours.
