Why this won't be AI slop

Four things every "AI content" tool skips.
We refuse to.

Agents have been burned. "Powered by AI" is a liability in 2026. Here's exactly what's inside the engine — not the marketing version.

𝗔
Audio that actually sounds like you
Every script is tagged with pacing and emphasis markers before the voice clone runs — where to breathe, where to pause, where to punch a word. Skip this step and your clone sounds like a GPS robot. We refuse to generate a single line of audio without it.
𝗩
Your voice, your phrasing
We build a per-agent voice model from your recording — cadence, filler words, the way you say "Menlo Park." Your avatar sounds like you, not a generic real-estate bot reading off a script.
𝗤
Humans check every video
Before anything publishes, a real person on our team reviews the audio and the final render — lip sync, emphasis, compliance. If anything looks off, it doesn't ship. AI writes, humans gatekeep.
⚖
Fair Housing, at generation time
The prompt itself is guardrailed against protected-class language. Not a spellcheck afterward — it can't be written wrong in the first place. Your license matters too much for "oops."

Honest limitations

What PropCast is not.

Read this before you pay us. If any of these are dealbreakers — walk away. We'd rather lose the sale than miss on expectations.

No. 01 ×

NOT a magic money button.

If you don’t have listings to talk about or a real point of view, PropCast just makes thin content scale faster. We fix distribution — not positioning. If you’re not already doing the fundamentals, more reach won’t save you.

No. 02 ×

NOT an AI clone pretending to be you.

Your face, your voice, your words. PropCast re-times and re-cuts what you actually said in your 6-minute recording. It does not fabricate quotes, invent opinions, or pretend you’ve toured a property you haven’t. Every hook is something you actually said.

No. 03 ×

NOT instant.

Day 0 you record. Days 1–2 we process, compliance-check, and queue. Day 3 your first post goes live. A full 30-day channel ships across the month. Anyone promising same-day AI content is either lying or skipping compliance — and one of those ends in a DRE complaint.

No. 04 ×

NOT fully hands-off.

Once a week you’ll get a 5-minute Loom: here are the 3 hooks going out this week, thumbs up or kill. That’s the only touchpoint we require. If you want absolute zero involvement in what goes out under your name — PropCast isn’t for you.

No. 05 ×

NOT a replacement for your CRM, door-knocking, or open houses.

Content compounds attention. It doesn’t close deals on its own. Your business still runs on relationships — PropCast just keeps the top of funnel from going dark while you work the bottom. Agents who expect leads without lifting the phone will be disappointed.
