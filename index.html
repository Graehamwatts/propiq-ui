Why this won't be AI slop

Four things every "AI content" tool skips.
We refuse to.

Agents have been burned. "Powered by AI" is a liability in 2026. Here's exactly what's inside the engine — not the marketing version.

𝗦
SSML voice control
Every script is tagged with speech synthesis markup — where to breathe, where to pause, where to emphasize. Without this, your clone sounds like a GPS robot. We refuse to generate a single line of audio without it.
𝗣
Your voice profile
We build a per-agent voice model from your recording — cadence, phrasing, filler words, the way you say "Menlo Park." Your avatar sounds like you. Not a generic real estate bot.
Q
Humans check every video
Before anything publishes, a real person on our team reviews the audio and the final video. Lip sync, emphasis, compliance. If something looks off — it doesn't ship.
⚖
Fair Housing, at generation time
The prompt itself is guardrailed against protected-class language. Not a spellcheck after — it can't be written wrong in the first place. Your career matters too much for "oops."
