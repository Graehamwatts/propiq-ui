Common Questions

Questions Agents Ask Before Starting

How long does setup take? ▼
New agents: Approximately 5 minutes. Answer brand voice questions, upload your logo and headshot, enter your zip codes, and record a quick video clip for your AI avatar. That's it — PropCast handles everything from there.

Already have an AI avatar or ElevenLabs voice? About 2 minutes. Connect your existing avatar, import your voice, and you're live. PropCast detects what you already have and skips the steps you don't need.
Will it look professional? ▼
Yes — and we don't just say that. Scroll up to see real examples of PropCast-generated content. Every video uses broadcast-quality AI generation with your personal avatar, your brand colors, professional motion graphics, and data overlays pulled from live market data. Captions are written in your voice, not generic templates.

Here's a quick comparison: Neighborhood Tour Example · Market Update Example · Listing Highlight Example

Most agents tell us the content looks better than what they were paying a videographer $500/video to produce.
What platforms does it post to? ▼
Instagram, Facebook, YouTube, and Google Business Profile. All four, automatically. Each post is optimized for the specific platform's format and algorithm.
What if my market is niche? ▼
PropCast works for any market, any property type, any brand voice. It pulls local MLS data and Google Trends specific to your zip codes and tailors every piece of content to your exact audience.
How much does it cost? ▼
Plans start at $197/month for Starter, $397/month for Growth (most popular), and $697/month for Dominate. All plans include a 14-day free trial. See full pricing details below.
Do I have to create any content myself? ▼
No. PropCast handles 90% of your content automatically. The only thing we never automate is your personal relationship moments — client wins, open house selfies, real-life stories. That 10% is yours, and it's actually more powerful because everything else is handled.
What happens to my content if I cancel? ▼
You own everything PropCast creates. Download it all anytime. It's your content, your brand, your data — we just build and distribute it for you.
Can I cancel anytime? ▼
Yes. No contracts, no penalties. Cancel from your dashboard with one click. We think you'll stay because the results speak for themselves.
